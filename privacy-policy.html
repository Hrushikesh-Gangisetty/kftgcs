Privacy Policy for Drone/GCS Application

Effective Date: 2026-02-26

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our drone and Ground Control Station (GCS) application.

Information We Collect

  • User Data: We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and account details when you register or interact with our application.
  • Flight Telemetry: The application collects data concerning the flight operations of your drone, including flight paths, altitude, speed, and other telemetry data necessary for safety and operational purposes.
  • Mission Information: We store details of your missions, including waypoints, mission objectives, and any specific instructions you provide within the application.

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide, maintain, and improve our application and services. We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To offer personalized experiences and services tailored to your needs.
  • To monitor and analyze the usage of our application to improve functionality and performance.
  • To ensure safety and security within the use of our drones and GCS.
  • To communicate with you, including responding to inquiries, sending updates, and providing support.

Data Protection Measures

We take the protection of your personal information seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure your data against unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in operating our application and services, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • To comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, and ensure the safety of our users and the public.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data under applicable laws. You may also have the right to withdraw consent for data processing where applicable.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
